# WathiqCare Legal Evidence Retention Policy

## Policy Statement

Medico-legal evidence generated by WathiqCare is retained as controlled legal record material. This includes refusal evidence, secure-link decisions, signature artifacts, PDFs, legal package outputs, and associated traceability metadata.

## Evidence Scope

- Final Arabic and English PDFs
- Legal package outputs and court-ready bundles
- Secure-link decision evidence and typed-name or signature attestations
- Signature proof metadata and certificate artifacts where available
- Supporting audit and audit-chain records needed to demonstrate provenance

## Retention Requirement

- Final legal evidence artifacts: retain permanently unless a stricter jurisdictional rule applies
- Supporting traceability metadata: retain permanently with the evidence set
- Operational copies used for troubleshooting: retain only as long as needed for the incident, then archive or purge under controlled procedure

## Preservation Rules

- Evidence artifacts must be stored in UTF-8-safe and integrity-preserving storage paths.
- Evidence files must not be overwritten without version control or explicit superseding record behavior.
- If regeneration occurs, prior versions must remain traceable.
- Any suspected corruption, truncation, or encoding defect in Arabic output is a high-severity incident.

## Access Control

- Access limited to authorized Legal Affairs, Medical Director delegates, Compliance, and approved IT administrators
- Demo or training use must rely on redacted or controlled non-production data where possible
- Exported evidence must be handled as confidential patient-related material

## Validation Expectation

Before relying on any evidence set for legal or executive review, confirm:

- file completeness
- Arabic rendering integrity
- associated audit references
- version identity
- source environment
